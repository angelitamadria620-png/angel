Festivals & Events

Experience the vibrant celebrations of Agoo

Feast of Our Lady of Charity

Marian processions and novenas at the Basilica Minore of Our Lady of Charity.

  • Month: May 1 (canonical coronation anniversary) and September 8 (Nativity of the Blessed Virgin Mary).
  • Duration: usually lasts 1 to 2 days
Simbang Gabi

Dawn masses leading to Christmas with traditional carols and family gatherings.

  • Month: December
  • Highlights: Simbang Gabi masses
  • Time: 4AM daily for 9 days
Holy Week

Good Friday Processions, Stations of the Cross, and solemn religious observances.

  • Month: March/April
  • Duration: 7 days (Palm Sunday to Easter Sunday)
Dinengdeng Festival

Traditional dances, music, and arts showcasing Ilocano heritage. Promotes healthy living, local farming, and Ilocano pride

  • Various Dates: About 1 week of activities
  • Features: vibrant street dancing, culinary competitions, cultural shows, and parades inspired by Ilocano vegetables, all centered around the traditional dish dinengdeng.
